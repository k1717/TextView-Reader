# Privacy

TextView Reader is designed as an offline local reader.

## Data collected

The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or account login.

## Local data

The app may store local reading-related data on the device, including:

- recent files;
- recent folders;
- reading position;
- bookmarks;
- theme settings;
- imported fonts;
- app preferences.

This data is used for app functionality and remains on the device unless the user manually exports, backs up, shares, or deletes it.

## Network

The app does not require a network connection for normal reading features.

## Permissions

Storage-related permissions are used to let the user browse and open local files. Notification permission may be used for reading progress notification behavior on Android versions that require runtime notification permission.
